WHY SESSION-BASED ANALYSIS IS CLINICALLY SUPERIOR TO CONTINUOUS PREDICTION

❌ Continuous Prediction (Old Approach)

Runs AI every 800ms on live data. One blink artifact spikes risk to 80%. No context window. Doctor sees a flashing number — not actionable evidence. Wastes CPU. Generates false alarms.

✅ Session Analysis (This Page)

Analyses the full recorded CSV in windowed segments. Detects sustained risk patterns. Filters transient artifacts by requiring risk to persist across multiple windows. Produces a timestamped evidence report.

📋 Clinical Proof for the Doctor

Provides FFT spectra per channel (downloadable PNG), a biomarker deviation table, and a structured written report with timestamps — evidence the doctor can file, discuss, and act on.
