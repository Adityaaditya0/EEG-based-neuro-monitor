Electrode Placement Guide
How to Wear the Headband
1

Clean Electrode Sites

Gently clean the forehead, temples, and earlobes with the alcohol prep pad. Ensure skin is dry before placement.

2

Position Frontal Electrodes

Place FP1 FP2 on the forehead, 2 cm above each eyebrow. Secure F7 F8 at the temples.

3

Attach Occipital Sensors

Slide the headband so O1 O2 sit at the back of the skull, just above the hairline.

4

Clip Ear References

Attach EAR-L EAR-R clips to each earlobe. These serve as the reference & ground electrodes.

5

Power On & Connect

Press the button on the ESP32 module. The green LED will blink until connected. Enter the device IP below and click Connect.

Start Session

Enter patient details and connect to your ESP32 device.

Device Configuration
Found on the ESP32 serial monitor or device display after WiFi connection.
